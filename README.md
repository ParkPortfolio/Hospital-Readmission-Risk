
# Hospital-Readmission-Risk
Healthcare analytics dashboard using 99,340 hospital encounters to identify patient groups at higher 30-day readmission risk. Built in Tableau to highlight KPIs, risk tiers, prior utilization, diagnostic complexity, and intervention opportunities for discharge planning and follow-up care.

## Business Question

Which patient groups show the highest readmission risk, and what utilization or clinical complexity patterns may help prioritize discharge planning and follow-up care?

## Dataset

The analysis uses hospital encounter-level data containing information related to readmission status, patient risk category, prior utilization, and diagnostic burden.

Raw data are not included in this repository if restricted by size, licensing, or privacy considerations.

## Key Metrics

- Total encounters: 99,340
- Overall 30-day readmission rate: 11.39%
- High-risk readmission rate: 26.97%

## Key Findings

- Readmission risk increased steadily across patient risk tiers, rising from 7% among low-risk patients to 27% among high-risk patients.
- Patients with greater prior utilization showed higher risk classifications.
- Patients with more recorded diagnoses had higher 30-day readmission rates.
- The combination of prior utilization and diagnostic complexity may help identify patients most appropriate for targeted discharge planning and follow-up intervention.

## Recommendation

Healthcare teams should prioritize elevated- and high-risk patients for post-discharge intervention, especially those with higher diagnostic complexity and greater prior utilization. These patients may benefit from earlier follow-up scheduling, medication review, care coordination, and discharge planning support.

## Dashboard Features

- KPI cards summarizing total encounters, overall readmission rate, and high-risk readmission rate.
- Risk-tier comparison showing readmission rates across patient groups.
- Prior utilization trend by readmission-risk category.
- Diagnostic burden analysis showing how clinical complexity relates to readmission.
- Heatmap combining diagnostic complexity and prior utilization.
